About Me

My name is Sarah. I'm originally from New York City and I recently moved to San Francisco to further pursue a career in the tech industry. I currently live in the East Bay with my boyfriend and our 8-month old puppy, Opie. As a stepping stone to furthering my career in tech, I'm currently attending UC Berkeley's Extension Coding Bootcamp which, thus far, has been a great learning experience.
